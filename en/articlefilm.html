OBLIVION: A FUTURISTIC VISION OF DRONES

In the film *Oblivion* by Joseph Kosinski, drones play a central role, embodying both protectors and threats. Dive into this science-fiction work that explores the implications of drone technology in a dystopian future. Through spectacular imagery and innovative technology, the film questions our relationship with machines and their potential for control.

SYNOPSIS

2077. Jack Harper, one of the last drone repair technicians stationed on Earth after its population was evacuated, is in charge of securing and recovering resources. Following decades of war against a terrifying alien force that devastated Earth, Jack is part of a massive operation to extract the last resources necessary for the survival of his people. His mission is nearing its end. In just two weeks, he will join the remaining survivors in a space colony thousands of kilometers away from this ravaged planet he now calls home.

Living and working at high altitude on what's left of Earth, Jack's "solitary" life is turned upside down when he witnesses the crash of a spaceship and decides to rescue its beautiful occupant. Feeling an immediate attraction and curiosity for Jack, this mysterious stranger will push him to question everything he thought he knew.

What he believed to be reality shatters when he confronts certain elements of his past that had been erased from his memory. Discovering a new mission, Jack is driven to make the ultimate sacrifice for humanity. He then understands that he is a man who must go far beyond what his known world could ever bring about.
