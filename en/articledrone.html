THE DRONE, A TECHNOLOGICAL REVOLUTION

The drone, also called UAV (Unmanned Aerial Vehicle), is an innovation that dates back to the early 20th century. Initially used for military purposes, it has gradually become more widespread, becoming an essential tool in fields as varied as surveillance, delivery, and medicine.

BUSINESS
